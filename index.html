Hi, we are team Lazaro

Matt and Stacia have served in the Stockton community as well known health and fitness consultants for over ten years. With over 5,000 clients and numerous success stories, Matt and Stacia take pride and honor in every one of their clients. Their motivation comes from seeing people feel better than ever, taking their health back and living vivaciously. When working with Lazaro Nutrition you will have your own personalized health and wellness program tailored to your specific goals.

Your program will address bettering your nutrition, training, rest and recovery, and other wellness aspects such as your mindset! If you feel the need for change in a positive direction, achieving optimal health inside and out let Matt and Stacia show you how to get started.

"I lost over 20 lbs and 10% body fat. My husband lost 30 lbs and 9% body fat."

Before starting with Matt, I would have called myself a fit person, but I knew I had some changes to make with my eating habits. I worked out 5 to 6 days a week, but if you looked at me I don’t think you could tell. My goal was to get lean and to start understanding the importance of eating the right foods. Matt helped me with that. He created a menu that was based on my goals, my body, and my likes and dislikes of food. Same as my friends, within a few weeks I noticed such amazing changes. I looked better, I felt better, I slept better and had more energy. It was such a change that even my husband set up his own appointment to see Matt. My results were everything I could ask for and more. I lost over 20 lbs and 10% body fat. My husband lost 30 lbs and 9% body fat. I now have people asking me where I workout, not “oh you workout?” The program/menu has now just become my way of life. Matt gave me the resources and knowledge to do this on my own. I thank him everyday for changing my life to a healthier and happier one.

Riina & Kevin
